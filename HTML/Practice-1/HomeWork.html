Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favoritre foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously irch scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium bear
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette
  5. Whe the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
